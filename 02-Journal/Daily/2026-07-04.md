# 💡 Key Learnings

Today's biggest takeaway was understanding that branches are extremely lightweight because they are simply pointers to commits rather than copies of the project.

I also learned that:

* HEAD points to the branch I am currently working on.
* Every branch can have its own independent commit history.
* Switching branches updates the working directory to match that branch.
* Visualizing commit history with `git log --graph --decorate --oneline --all` makes it much easier to understand Git.
